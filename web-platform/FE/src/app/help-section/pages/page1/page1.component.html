🩺 Medical System Dashboard

This screen is part of the Medical System, a secure platform designed to manage and monitor critical healthcare data. The interface presents two essential entry points:

👩‍⚕️ Doctor List
Provides an overview of all registered doctors. It allows filtering, searching, and managing profiles, specialties, and availability.

🕵️ Audit Trail
Ensures complete visibility of user actions within the system. It captures who did what and when, for transparency and compliance.
