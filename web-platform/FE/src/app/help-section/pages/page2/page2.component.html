🩺 Patient List Page

🏁 1. Accessing the Patients List

After logging in, navigate to the Patients List section.
The page displays a structured table with details of all registered patients.

🔍 2. Searching for a Patient

Use the search bar to find a patient by name.
Begin typing the patient’s first or last name — the list will automatically filter results in real time.

📋 3. Viewing Patient Details

Click on a patient’s name in the table to access their full profile.
You will see the patient’s personal information, clinical records, alerts, and more.

⚕️ 4. Patient Actions (Quick Access Icons)

Each patient row has a set of action icons that let you manage medical data:

  • 💊 Medications – View and update the patient's medication list.
  • 🤧 Allergies – View and manage known allergies.
  • 📄 EMR – Access the electronic medical record.
  • 👨‍⚕️ Consultations – Review past consultations and add new ones.
  • 💬 Recommendations – Add or view treatment recommendations.
  • 🏥 Investigations/Referrals – Manage referrals and investigations.
  • 📈 Charts – Visualize patient data (e.g., vitals, trends).

➕ Adding a New Patient Click the “Add new patient” button at the top right of the page. Fill in the required patient details in the form. Click Save to add the patient to the system.
